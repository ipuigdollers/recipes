## Ingredients
- Box of velveeta shells & cheese
- Can of bay salmon
- Can of peas
- Can of beans
- A large quantity of Hot Sauce

## Directions

Cook shells & cheese according to package directions. Drain tuna and peas well. Gently stir into shells & cheese.
